Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

The Black Soldier Fly (Hermetia illucens) is a harmless, non-pest insect. In its larval stage, it is an incredibly efficient bio-converter, rapidly consuming organic waste and transforming it into high-value protein and fat.

We process a wide range of clean organic waste, including central-market produce waste, restaurant and hotel residuals, food-processing by-products, agricultural residues, and select animal by-products.

Our rearing cycle is extremely rapid, typically taking only 10 to 14 days for the larvae to fully process the feedstock and reach harvest size.

Yes. All Ecovera products are 100% natural, processed under controlled conditions, and completely free from artificial hormones and antibiotics.

We serve the feed sector (poultry farms, aquaculture, pet food), agriculture (organic farming, fertilizer companies), and provide waste management services for municipalities and food companies.

Ecovera is currently operational in Jordan, with strategic plans for geographic expansion into Egypt and the wider Gulf region.
