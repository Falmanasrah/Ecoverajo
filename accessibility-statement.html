Accessibility Statement

Our Commitment

Ecovera is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for everyone. We aim to make our website (ecoverajo.com) accessible and user-friendly for all visitors, regardless of technology or ability.

Continuous Improvement

We are continuously working to improve the user experience for everyone and apply the relevant accessibility standards to the best of our capabilities.

Feedback and Assistance

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you experience any accessibility problems or require assistance navigating the site or accessing its content, please do not hesitate to contact us. We will make all reasonable efforts to make the page accessible or provide the information in a format that meets your needs.
